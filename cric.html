Represented Bennett in cricket

Cricket helped me to be physically fit and mentally strong and also created discipline in my life and Since a sport requires obedience towards a goal, it enlisted hard work in me. And also sports teaches you real-life lessons like how to face the pressure situations and also in a sport you will go through a difficult phase like injuries as I got a blood clot in my left hip when I slipped and fell down while bowling, it took me around 4 months to recover and after facing these difficult situations i learned that whenever you phase this you need to grow through it rather than going through it.

And our team coach Vikrant sir helped me to change my mindset and its a great privilege to represent the university because you are not playing for fun, you are carrying the responsibility. there are a lot of unforgettable memories with this great team, at last, you need to love the journey more than results.
